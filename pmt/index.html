Zalo PRODUCT MANAGEMENT TRAINEE Program

How many candidates are selected?

We are looking for 15 bright minds. However, in order to ensure the program quality, there is no official quota

What will be gained through training program?

"Zalo PMT offers hands-on training and close mentorship during the program. The training process includes 2 phases:

1/ Introduction - Base Knowledge (1.5 months): Trainees will be facilitated with a series of training sessions such as the basis of Product Management, Design Thinking, User Empathy, Prototype and Test, and other teamwork assignments.

2/ On-the-job Training (4.5 months): Trainees will be assigned to a specific Product team and work under direct supervision of a mentor to learn and develop skills through hands-on experience. There will be monthly reviews with respective mentors to make sure trainees are growing and developing in their careers."

Are trainees able to choose a specific Zalo product to work on it?

During the competency interview, a candidate's preference is noted and paired with their experience and skill level. Mentors will then assign a specific Zalo product that is the best match.

What's next after the program?

Upon graduation, trainees will be paired with their experience and skills level to identify and join a product team as a full-time Product Owner.
